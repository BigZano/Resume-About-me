Boy howdy it's been a long time. Lots has changed, lots has improved, it's been a fun 6-ish months since the last update. Imagine being so busy I kept forgetting to update my first and probably most long standing project. That brings me to the source of that frenzy, the armory bot. It was a long, long time in the making. I ended up taking the bot that just handled voice chats all the way through to a full service moderation bot. It handles automodding via a very finicky regex, I have an economy and RPG system that supplies a storefront, items, and two different pathways for admins to create "deployments" or other thematic 40K events for Space Marine 2. It does things like hello message embeds, it handles all sorts of goodies. I also onboarded an AI agent to serve as a mission prompt generator and a comic relief by joking with and roasting users. It's easily the most ambitions and largest project I've done. I utilized Claude for the CSS and the Javascript on the front end, as to be completely honest I can only learn so much at once, and I'm already going for my Bachelors in IT while learning Golang and Python as well. It's also my first paid product! The subscription is only ever going to be $5USD, and it's going to cover all paid features. So far, the only paid feature is the AI agent, with the music player bot coming in the future.

I lean pretty heavily on the SM2 and WH40K theme throughout, even with the day / night toggle being Salamanders / Mechanicus themes. I'm a massive fan of both factions, so it's only natural. Who knows, maybe the next one with be Custodian gold and I'll finally hit all 3 favorites. Tell your friends, share the link, and fill out any issues or request any features!
